# Data Sources & Attribution

This document lists all data sources used in the OpenGov Sri Lanka platform to ensure transparency and credibility.

## Economic Indicators

### National Debt
- **Source**: Ministry of Finance, Sri Lanka & Central Bank of Sri Lanka
- **Data**: Total public debt of USD 106.17 billion (as of December 31, 2024)
  - Government debt: USD 101.25 billion
  - SOE guaranteed debt: USD 4.90 billion
- **Reference**: [Treasury.gov.lk - Public Debt](https://www.treasury.gov.lk/web/guest/public-debt)
- **Last Updated**: December 2024

### Inflation Rate
- **Source**: Central Bank of Sri Lanka (CBSL)
- **Data**: 2.1% annual inflation (Colombo, December 2025)
- **Reference**: [CBSL - Consumer Price Indices](https://www.cbsl.gov.lk/en/statistics/statistical-tables/real-sector)
- **Last Updated**: December 2025

### GDP Growth
- **Source**: Central Bank of Sri Lanka & Department of Census and Statistics
- **Data**: 4.5% GDP growth (2024-2025 projection)
- **Reference**: [CBSL - Economic Indicators](https://www.cbsl.gov.lk/en/statistics/economic-indicators)
- **Last Updated**: 2024

### Foreign Exchange Reserves
- **Source**: Central Bank of Sri Lanka
- **Data**: Approximately USD 5.8 billion (January 2026 estimate)
- **Reference**: [CBSL - Official Reserve Assets](https://www.cbsl.gov.lk/en/statistics/statistical-tables/external-sector)
- **Last Updated**: January 2026

### Exchange Rates
- **Source**: Central Bank of Sri Lanka
- **Data**: Daily indicative exchange rates for USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, INR
- **Reference**: [CBSL - Exchange Rates](https://www.cbsl.gov.lk/en/rates-and-indicators/exchange-rates)
- **Last Updated**: Daily

## Development Projects

### Major Infrastructure Projects
- **Source**: Ministry of Finance - Department of Project Management and Monitoring
- **Data**: 171 large-scale development projects (2024)
- **Reference**: [Treasury - Progress of Mega Scale Development Projects](https://www.treasury.gov.lk/web/guest/publications)
- **Projects Include**:
  - Central Expressway (Section 3) - MCC & CCECC Consortium
  - Bandaranaike Airport Terminal 2 - Taisei Corporation
  - Port City Financial Centre - CHEC
  - Mannar Wind Power Project - Adani Green Energy
  - Greater Colombo Water & Wastewater - JICA-funded
  - Rural Road Development Phase IV

### Budget Allocations
- **Source**: Ministry of Finance - Budget 2024
- **Data**: Rs. 1,260 billion allocated for development activities (4% of GDP)
- **Reference**: [Budget 2024 Proposals](https://www.treasury.gov.lk/web/guest/budget)
- **Last Updated**: 2024

## Debt & Loans

### IMF Program
- **Source**: International Monetary Fund
- **Data**: USD 2.9 billion Extended Fund Facility (EFF)
- **Reference**: [IMF - Sri Lanka Program](https://www.imf.org/en/Countries/LKA)
- **Last Updated**: 2024

### Multilateral Debt
- **Source**: Ministry of Finance
- **Data**: 
  - Asian Development Bank: USD 12.9 billion
  - World Bank/IDA: USD 6.2 billion
- **Reference**: [Treasury - External Debt](https://www.treasury.gov.lk/web/guest/public-debt)

### Bilateral Debt
- **Source**: Ministry of Finance
- **Data**: USD 10.75 billion (China, India, Japan, others)
- **Reference**: [Treasury - Public Debt Reports](https://www.treasury.gov.lk/web/guest/public-debt)

### Sovereign Bonds
- **Source**: Ministry of Finance
- **Data**: USD 12.5 billion in restructured International Sovereign Bonds
- **Reference**: [Debt Restructuring Agreement - December 2024](https://www.treasury.gov.lk/)

## Parliament & MPs

### Members of Parliament
- **Source**: Parliament of Sri Lanka
- **Data**: Directory of current MPs, constituencies, party affiliations
- **Reference**: [Parliament.lk - Directory of Members](https://www.parliament.lk/en/members-of-parliament/directory-of-members)
- **Note**: MP data in the platform uses representative samples for demonstration purposes

## Historical Timeline

### Economic Crisis & Recovery
- **Sources**: 
  - Central Bank Annual Reports
  - Ministry of Finance Publications
  - IMF Country Reports
  - News archives from reputable sources
- **Events**: 2010-2024 major economic and political milestones
- **Reference**: [CBSL - Annual Reports](https://www.cbsl.gov.lk/en/publications/economic-and-financial-reports/annual-reports)

## Data Refresh Policy

- **Economic Indicators**: Updated monthly or as new official data is released
- **Exchange Rates**: Can be updated daily via CBSL API (when available)
- **Development Projects**: Quarterly updates based on government reports
- **MP Information**: Updated after elections or major changes
- **News Items**: Updated weekly with verified information

## API Access

The platform aims to integrate with official APIs when available:
- **Parliament API**: [parliament.lk](https://www.parliament.lk/) - Registration required
- **CBSL Data Library**: [cbsl.gov.lk](https://www.cbsl.gov.lk/en/statistics/economic-data-library)
- **Open Data Portal**: [data.gov.lk](https://www.data.gov.lk/)

## Disclaimer

All data is sourced from official government publications and reputable international organizations. While we strive for accuracy, users should verify critical information with primary sources. This platform is for informational and educational purposes.
